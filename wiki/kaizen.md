There is an opportunity for kaizen, or constant self-improvment. There are many articles on best practices for building LLMs themselves. Topics here include how to prime or pre-prompt an LLM for best results. Approaches for devising LLM memory, context and recall (The twitter user @karpathy for example has been a constant source of fresh thinking here).
